# Secure Evidence Workflow Design Partners

Problem: Commercial validation requires paid demand for independently verifiable evidence, not enthusiasm for a refund demo or cryptographic receipts.

Acceptance:

- Seek two paid pilots for any qualified consequential-action profile.
- Record buyer, external evidence consumer, costly failure, existing evidence, required capture boundary, budget, integration scope, and security/procurement requirements.
- Test whether independent verification materially improves the buyer's ordinary logs.
- Test willingness to install an enforced side-effect-boundary adapter.
- Keep pilot pricing above commodity log storage and identify a credible annual budget.
- Do not require refund pilots.

Verification:

- Two paid commitments or an explicit failed-demand finding exist.
- At least one external evidence consumer agrees to assess an offline package.
- Pilot scope maps cleanly to a profile and adapter rather than core forks.
