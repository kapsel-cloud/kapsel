# Operate A First-Party Transparency Service

Problem: A Kapsel-operated witness adds substantial cryptographic, operational, key-custody, uptime, and liability scope without validated need.

Acceptance:

- Cite customer requirements for residency, private deployment, independence, or assurance unavailable from selected backends.
- Remain SCITT-compatible and independently verifiable.
- Define append-only/non-equivocation model, key operations, privacy allowlists, recovery, SLOs, and incident response.
- Complete production-assurance and independent security review gates.
- Keep service storage choices out of collector/verifier dependencies.

Verification:

- Reactivation ADR documents demand and rejected alternatives.
- Independent clients verify issued receipts.

## Next action

Remain deferred until commercial evidence requires Kapsel to operate the service.
