# Add Multi-Witness And Monitoring Assurance

Problem: Signatures alone do not prevent a transparency service from presenting split views, but V1 should not build a monitoring ecosystem before counterparties require it.

Acceptance:

- Cite customer need for non-equivocation assurance beyond the selected backend's guarantees.
- Prefer standardized pinned prior state, independent monitors, gossip, cross-logging, or multiple backends.
- Define disagreement, compromise, and recovery semantics without incompatible receipts.
- Preserve explicit `not_checked` reporting when monitor/prior evidence is absent.

Verification:

- Split-view scenarios are tested end to end with independent operators/implementations where claims require them.
- Reports never infer non-equivocation from a signature alone.

## Next action

Remain deferred until external consumers demand this assurance.
